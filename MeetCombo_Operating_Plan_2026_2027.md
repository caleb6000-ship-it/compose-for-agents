# MeetCombo 2026-2027 Operating Plan

**Start:** July 16, 2026  
**Core thesis:** MeetCombo is an evidence engine and AI operating system that validates businesses virtually before money is invested.

## Connected engines

1. **Toyota Sales:** stable cash flow and immediate lead-generation ROI.
2. **Raised Outside:** founder-led outdoor content, products, fatherhood, and audience.
3. **MeetCombo:** observe, think, simulate, launch, operate, improve.
4. **Virtual Fleet:** first complete case study proving the model.

## 90-day virtual fleet

**July 16-October 13, 2026**

- Toyota Sienna LE/XLE
- 2024 Tacoma SR 4x4
- Camry Hybrid
- GR Supra 3.0 automatic

Track pricing, availability, trips, reviews, delivery, mileage, purchase economics, insurance, platform costs, detailing, maintenance, depreciation, labor, events, seasonality, and social demand. Classify each simulated booking as Likely Won, Competitive, Unlikely, or Insufficient Evidence.

## Purchase gate

- 15+ realistically bookable days per month
- Positive cash flow after all costs
- Survives a 20% booking reduction
- Repeatable delivery/detailing workflow
- Three months of fixed expenses plus deductible, tires, and operating reserve
- Attractive annual cash-on-cash return, margin, and payback period
- Written lender, warranty, commercial-use, and insurance confirmation
